Desktop: right click or 'two finger click' and select 'save as' in the menu that follows. The article will be saved as an '.html' file on your computer. You can also use pocket, readingqueue, instapaper, or the Reading List cloud storage in safari for offline reading.

Mobile: hit the share button and try a mobile article saving application like pocket, readingqueue, instapaper, or (if you have an ios device) use the Reading List cloud storage for offline reading.
